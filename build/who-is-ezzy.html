I aspire to inspire because this world is coming to an end. I see humanity disintegrate before my eyes every single day. In the environment in which I reside, there lies the over-saturation of hate, tension, and evil. Like many rap artists, I hail from the pit of the slums, but unlike many rap artists, I choose to fight against the urges to sin and use my gift for the wellbeing of mankind. This being said, not all of mankind likes hip-hop. Therefore I express my message through means of almost all aspects of art including graphic design, fashion design, audio visuals, and of course, music. My love for people has been instilled within me since birth and my love for art came shortly after. Throughout my lifetime, these two aspects of love merged into one, along with the desire to do wrong, the difficulty of doing right, the pressure of supporting my family, my religious beliefs, my love for what money can bring, and a few bad kids who just so happen to be my best friends. This is what I am a product of. I’m not a product of the world. I am a product of my life, my daily decisions, and my conscious. This is who I am, and this is Everything Ezzy. Remember, evil doesn’t discriminate against the rich or poor, black or white, Christian or Satanist. Adjust your priorities.

Love,

Ezzy
